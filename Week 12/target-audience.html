The Audience

The company of "Temple Inn & Suites" are targeting audiences who are temple workers, patrons, or families who do work in the temples, to provide them a hotel. In the hotel, service will be provided to our customers such as free access to chnaging rooms, a playroom and short-term babysitting services, breakfast, workout room, parking, temple shuttle, and more!
